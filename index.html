Frequently Asked Questions

How do you protect my intellectual property (IP)?▼

Protecting your IP is our top priority. We execute a comprehensive Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before any sensitive technical details are shared. All communication and data are handled through secure channels.

What happens if my hardware breaks during a test?▼

Our process is designed to minimize this risk through mandatory pre-test checks. However, in the event of a failure, our policy is transparent. We will immediately halt the test, document the event, and consult with you. If the issue is with the hardware, we can offer engineering support. If it's a facility issue, we take full responsibility according to our service agreement.

What about shipping and insurance?▼

We manage all logistics. Our service operates on FOB (Free on Board) terms, meaning our responsibility begins when your hardware is safely received at the test facility. We partner with trusted logistics providers and have liability insurance to protect your hardware while it's in our care.
